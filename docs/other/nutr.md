
## Biotin (Vitamin B7)

| **Item**                    | **Details**                                                                                                                                             |
|----------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Recommended Daily Dose** | 30 µg                                                                                                                                                    |
| **Why You Need It**        | Vital for energy metabolism, healthy skin, hair, and nails—especially important if you’re stressed or notice hair/skin issues.                          |
| **Top Food Sources**       | - Eggs (cooked, 1): ~10 µg<br>- Almonds (¼ cup): ~1.5 µg<br>- Sweet potatoes (1 medium): ~2.4 µg<br>- Sunflower seeds (¼ cup): ~2.6 µg<br>- Mushrooms (1 cup): ~5.6 µg<br>- Cheese (1 oz): ~0.4 µg<br>- Peanuts (½ cup): ~4.9 µg |
| **Alternative**            | Include multiple biotin-rich foods in daily meals                                                                                                       |
| **Supplement**             | 30 µg daily if diet is lacking                                                                                                                           |

## Quick Programmer Tips

- **Meal Prep**  
  Cook proteins, grains, and veggies in advance to save time.
- **Healthy Snacks**  
  Keep nuts, seeds, or fruit at your desk to avoid junk food.
- **Hydration**  
  Aim for 2–3 liters of water daily to maintain focus and reduce fatigue.
- **Movement Breaks**  
  Take short walks or stretch every couple of hours to improve circulation and relieve stress.
